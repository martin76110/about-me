Drop your song files here. The page is a small playlist — use the on-screen
left/right arrows to flip between songs. Each song needs a cover + an audio file:

  SONG 1 — "sometimes" (my bloody valentine)
    loveless.jpg        — album cover art (square works best, e.g. 1000x1000)
    loveless.mp3        — audio track (loops; fades in from low to full)

  SONG 2 — "stars" (hum)
    little-dipper.jpg   — album cover art (square)
    little-dipper.mp3   — audio track (loops)

Until a cover .jpg exists, that song shows a tinted placeholder cover.
Until an .mp3 exists, the visuals run silently for that song (no error).

To add/rename/reorder songs, edit the TRACKS array near the top of main.js.
