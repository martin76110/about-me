Drop your two files here, named exactly:

  loveless.jpg   — the album cover art (square works best, e.g. 1000x1000)
  loveless.mp3   — the audio track (loops; fades in from low to full)

Until loveless.jpg exists, the page shows a pink "loveless" placeholder cover.
Until loveless.mp3 exists, the visuals run silently (no error).

To change filenames, edit COVER_URL in main.js and the <audio> src in index.html.
